§ 01
01 — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

One screener. Every chain. One token.

SURCHI is a next-generation decentralized trading intelligence platform designed to provide professional-grade blockchain analytics, real-time market screening, wallet intelligence, AI-powered security analysis, and cross-chain trading discovery.

Unlike traditional DEX screeners that primarily display token prices and charts, SURCHI combines live blockchain indexing, advanced analytics, AI-driven security insights, and community collaboration into a single ecosystem — built to help traders, investors, developers, and researchers make informed decisions using accurate on-chain data across multiple blockchain networks.

A key distinction: the SURCHI platform is multi-chain — tracking tokens, liquidity, and wallets across Ethereum, BNB Chain, Base, Solana, Avalanche, Arbitrum, and Polygon. The $SURCHI token itself launches natively on Solana as an SPL token, chosen for its speed, low fees, and deep DeFi liquidity.
